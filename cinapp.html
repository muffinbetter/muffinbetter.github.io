Cinnamon Apple Cookies

Effort: Easy

15 mins (Prep), 14 mins (Bake)

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients

  • 1 cup oatmeal
  • ¾ all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 stick (8 tablespoons) butter
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Wet Ingredients

  • ½ of a large, red apple
  • 1 egg
  • ⅓ cup maple syrup
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Farenheit.
  2. Place stick of butter on counter so that it reaches room temperature.
  3. Dice half of the apple.
  4. Whip eggs and butter together first. Then combine all ingredients (other than apples) together in mixing bowl.
  5. Mix in apples.
  6. Scoop tablespoon-sized portions onto baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 14 minutes until golden brown.
